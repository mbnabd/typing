What is professional typing?

Professional typing as said; It is different from normal types. For example: Many people need a typist to type their manuscripts to print their books. For this purpose, it is not only the typing of those texts that is considered, and in addition to that, before the desired book is printed, all the stages and elements of page layout of that book must be done in a completely professional manner, observing all the writing points and... It was done in order to deliver the best and most professional typefaces according to the needs of customers and users.
